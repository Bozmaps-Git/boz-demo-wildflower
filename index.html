Our story

A little studio off Margaret's Buildings, built on a love of the seasons.

Wildflower & Fern began in 2014 when our founder, Lottie, swapped a desk job for a flower bucket and a market stall on Walcot Street. What started as Saturday posies has grown into a much-loved studio tucked just off Margaret's Buildings — but the heart of it hasn't changed.

We work almost entirely with British flower growers, including our own little plot just outside the city. That means our flowers travel a handful of miles, not thousands. They smell of the garden, they change with the months, and no two bunches are ever quite the same.

Whether it's a £28 letterbox posy or the flowers for your wedding day, every stem is chosen and tied by hand, right here in Bath.

Weddings & events

The flowers for your most-loved day

From an intimate registry-office posy to a full marquee of meadow blooms, we design wedding flowers that feel personal, seasonal and beautifully un-fussy. We only take a handful of weddings each month, so every couple gets our full attention.

  • A seasonal proposal & mood board tailored to you
  • Bouquets, buttonholes, arches, urns & table flowers
